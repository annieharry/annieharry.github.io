How to get there

Train

The line between Exeter and Dawlish is now open!

Trains run regularly from London Paddington and Manchester Picaddily direct to Truro.

Car

Take the M5 to Exeter and then join the A30.
Stay on the A30 until signs to St. Agnes.

From A30 (Devon) or A390 (Truro)

Follow signposts towards St. Agnes, B3277.
After 1.7 miles turn left towards Porthtowan.
After 2.3 miles at the bottom of the hill turn right onto Beach Rd.

Drive towards the sea and pass a village hall and a small car park, beyond this is the larger public car park.
Park here (£5.80/day) and walk towards the sea.
The Blue Bar will be on your right just before the beach.

From A30 (Lands End)

Take the exit towards Redruth/Scorrier.
Head towards Chapel Hill and follow signs to Porthtowan.
After 2.7 miles before a hill turn left onto Beach Rd.

Drive towards the sea and pass a village hall and a small car park, beyond this is the larger public car park.
Park here (£5.80/day) and walk towards the sea.
The Blue Bar will be on your right just before the beach.
